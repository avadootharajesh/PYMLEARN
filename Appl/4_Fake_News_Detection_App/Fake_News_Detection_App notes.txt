Fake_News_Detection_App notes
📌 What is Fake News Detection?
Classifying news articles or social media posts as real or fake (misinformation)

Helps improve information reliability, reduces spread of false info

🔍 How It Works
Use labeled datasets of news articles (fake/real)

Text preprocessing: clean, tokenize, remove stopwords

Feature extraction: TF-IDF, word embeddings (Word2Vec, BERT)

Train classifiers: Logistic Regression, SVM, Random Forest, or deep learning (LSTM, transformers)

Evaluate with accuracy, precision, recall, F1-score

⚙️ Key Challenges
Highly imbalanced and noisy data

Language nuances and sarcasm

Need for domain knowledge and fact-checking

Evolving misinformation tactics

🧰 Common Datasets
LIAR dataset

FakeNewsNet
