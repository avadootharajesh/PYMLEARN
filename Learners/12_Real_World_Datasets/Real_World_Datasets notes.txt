üìå What are Real World Datasets?
Datasets collected from actual environments ‚Äî business, healthcare, finance, social media, sensors, etc. ‚Äî often messy, complex, and large-scale.

üîç Characteristics
Noisy & Incomplete: Missing values, errors, inconsistent formatting

High Dimensionality: Many features, sometimes irrelevant

Unbalanced Classes: Some categories underrepresented

Heterogeneous: Mixed types (numerical, categorical, text, images)

Large Scale: Millions of rows or gigabytes of data

‚öôÔ∏è Importance
Train models that generalize well

Realistic evaluation of algorithms

Insights that can impact real decisions

Benchmarking performance on common tasks

üîß Tips for Working with Real World Datasets
Spend significant time on Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

Understand data collection process and domain context

Use sampling if dataset is too big initially

Handle missing data with imputation or domain knowledge

Feature engineering is crucial to capture important signals

Visualize data for anomalies and patterns

Use stratified splits for classification tasks to handle class imbalance
